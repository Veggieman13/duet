Duet — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Duet is a period and cycle tracking app. This policy explains what happens with your information when you use it. The short version: your data never leaves your phone — unless you choose to share it with your partner.

What data Duet handles

Duet lets you log information about your menstrual cycle: period dates, flow, symptoms, and personal notes, plus your typical cycle settings.

Where that data is stored

All of it is stored only on your device, in the app's local storage.

  • Duet has no user accounts — we could not identify you if we wanted to.
  • Duet has no servers — nothing you log is uploaded, transmitted, or backed up anywhere.
  • Duet contains no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.
  • Duet does not share any data with third parties — nothing is collected, so there is nothing to share.

Data deletion

You are in full control:

  • Delete any single day's log inside the app.
  • Delete everything at once via Settings → Delete all data.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all of its data from your device.

Health disclaimer

Duet provides general wellness information and cycle estimates. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and its predictions must not be relied on as contraception. For medical concerns, consult a healthcare professional.

Children

Duet is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from anyone (see above — we collect no data at all).

Partner sharing (optional)

Duet lets you optionally share your cycle with one partner. If — and only if — you turn this on:

  • Your cycle data (period dates, symptoms, notes and cycle settings) is stored on Duet's sync server so your partner's phone can read it. It is encrypted in transit.
  • The data is stored under a random identifier. It is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any account — Duet still has none of those.
  • Only the one partner who used your invite code can read it. Partners can read, never change.
  • Either of you can stop sharing at any time, which deletes the shared data from the server.
  • Nothing else changes: still no ads, no analytics, no third-party sharing.

If you never turn on partner sharing, no data leaves your device at all.
