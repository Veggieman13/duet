Delete your data in Duet

This page explains how to delete the data handled by Duet: Period & Cycle Tracker (developer: M. Groenteman).

How to delete your data

  1. Delete everything on your device: open Duet → Settings → Delete all data. This permanently removes all periods, symptoms, and notes stored on your phone. Uninstalling the app has the same effect.
  2. Delete shared data from the server: if you use partner sharing, open Duet → Settings → Partner sharing → Stop sharing. This immediately and permanently deletes your shared cycle data from Duet's sync server. Either partner can do this.
  3. Delete a single day: tap the day in the calendar and choose Clear this day.

What is deleted, what is kept

  • Deleted: all cycle data — period dates, flow, symptoms, notes, and cycle settings — from your device and, if sharing was on, from the sync server. Deletion is immediate; there is no additional retention period.
  • Kept: nothing. Duet has no accounts and stores no other data about you (no name, email, or identifiers linked to you).
