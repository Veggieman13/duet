# Duet — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 12, 2026**

Duet is a period and cycle tracking app. This policy explains what happens with
your information when you use it. The short version: **your data never leaves
your phone.**

## What data Duet handles

Duet lets you log information about your menstrual cycle: period dates, flow,
symptoms, and personal notes, plus your typical cycle settings.

## Where that data is stored

All of it is stored **only on your device**, in the app's local storage.

- Duet has **no user accounts** — we could not identify you if we wanted to.
- Duet has **no servers** — nothing you log is uploaded, transmitted, or backed
  up anywhere.
- Duet contains **no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking** of any kind.
- Duet does **not share any data with third parties** — nothing is collected,
  so there is nothing to share.

## Data deletion

You are in full control:

- Delete any single day's log inside the app.
- Delete everything at once via **Settings → Delete all data**.
- Uninstalling the app removes all of its data from your device.

## Health disclaimer

Duet provides general wellness information and cycle estimates. It is not a
medical device, does not provide medical advice, and its predictions must not
be relied on as contraception. For medical concerns, consult a healthcare
professional.

## Children

Duet is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any
data from anyone (see above — we collect no data at all).

## Future features

If a future version of Duet introduces optional features that require data to
leave your device (such as sharing your cycle with a partner), this policy will
be updated first, and any such feature will be strictly opt-in.

## Changes to this policy

Any updates to this policy will be published at this same address, with an
