## Full description (max 4000 chars)

Duet is a simple, beautiful period and ovulation tracker with one big
difference: your data is nobody's business but yours. Everything you log stays
on your phone — no account, no cloud, no ads, no tracking.

TRACK YOUR CYCLE
• Log your period with flow levels, symptoms, and personal notes
• See exactly where you are in your cycle, every day
• Predictions for your next period, fertile window, and ovulation
• Predictions get smarter as you log — Duet learns your real rhythm, not a
  textbook average

UNDERSTAND YOUR BODY
• A clear calendar showing logged and predicted days at a glance
• Tips for every phase of your cycle: energy, mood, food, and exercise
• Gentle guidance instead of medical jargon

TRULY PRIVATE
• All data is stored only on your device
• No sign-up, no email, no password — open the app and start
• No analytics, no advertising, no third parties
• Delete everything at any time with one tap

COMING SOON
• Partner sharing: let your partner follow your cycle from their own phone —
  because it takes two to duet.

Duet provides general wellness information, not medical advice. Cycle
predictions are estimates and should not be used as contraception.
